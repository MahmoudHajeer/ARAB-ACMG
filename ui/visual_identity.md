# BioTrust Lab – Visual Identity Guide

---

## 1. Core Concept

**Shield + Liquid + Cells**

### Symbolism

- **Shield** → Protection, Trust, Safety  
- **Liquid Wave** → Science, Laboratory, Biotechnology  
- **Cell Dots** → Microbiology, Molecular Precision, Diagnostics  
- **Red + Teal Contrast** → Clinical Authority + Modern Innovation  

**Overall Positioning:**  
Medical-grade trust with biotech innovation.

---

## 2. Primary Color Palette

### Primary Red

- HEX: `#D62C2C`
- RGB: (214, 44, 44)

**Usage:**
- “BIO”
- Shield outline
- “LAB”

**Meaning:**
- Authority
- Clinical seriousness
- Bold, confident identity

---

### Primary Teal

- HEX: `#1FA7AD`
- RGB: (31, 167, 173)

**Usage:**
- Lower shield section
- “TRUST”
- Arabic typography

**Meaning:**
- Scientific calm
- Biotech innovation
- Modern trust signal

---

### Background Color

- HEX: `#F4F4F4`
- Clean light neutral
- Medical-grade clarity

---

## 3. Secondary Accent

### Light Teal Highlight

- HEX: `#36BFC4`

**Recommended Use:**
- Gradients
- Hover states
- UI transitions
- Subtle depth layers

## 4. Typography Direction

### English Font Style

**Suggested Alternatives:**

## 5. Brand Personality

**Industry:** Biotechnology & Clinical Laboratory  
**Tone:** Clean, high-trust, research-grade  
**Positioning:** Advanced diagnostics & molecular-level precision  

### Brand Traits
- Protective
- Scientific
- Structured
- Reliable
- Modern
- Not playful
- Not decorative

```
Brand Identity:
Industry: Biotechnology & Clinical Laboratory
Personality: Trustworthy, scientific, modern, protective
Visual Core: Shield + fluid science + micro cells
Primary Colors: #D62C2C (clinical red), #1FA7AD (scientific teal)

```
